All About Me

Hello everyone! My name is Kylee Koch and I am a senior at the University of Montana

I am studying Media Arts and pursuing my BA online through the University.

I particularly enjoy focusing on both Illustrator and Photoshop to create my work,

but I also have grown fond of web design and motion design.

I currently work two jobs, one being at an after school program in Missoula,

and the othe job is freelance design for a magazine company located in Billings, Montana.

I have a strong passion for both of these jobs, and look forward to my future as an artist.

Thank you for taking the time to check out my page, I hope you enjoy!

Why Me?

You may be wondering why you should check out my work, or what makes me special

compared to others, so let me tell you a little more about my experiences with

media arts and my work. I get my most inspiration from ideas that other people have.

I love hearing what other people want, and then putting my own twist on it to make it unique.

I will be honest, I am not going to be the most experienced designer you could contact,

but I can promise that I will work hard to learn and achieve whatever it is that the customer

wants. I enjoy challenging myself, and am willing to do whatever it takes to make my work

pleasing to the viewer. If there are any other questions you may have, feel free to contact me!
