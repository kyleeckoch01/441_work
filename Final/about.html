About Me

Hello there everyone! My name is Kylee Koch and I am currently a senior
Media Arts major at the University of Montana. Art is my passion, so whenever
I have any free time, I am most likely working on some sort of project on my computer.
I specialize in drawings made through Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop.
I have some experience in web design, as well as motion design and video editing.
I enjoy challenging myself and stepping out of my comfort zone, so I am always
willing to try and learn new things. If you are interested in viewing some of my
work, head on over to the gallery page and check out what I have been working on.
